Source: NIH 1R21MH118556 Nikolaidis (PI)
Dates: 12/20/2018 – 12/20/2022
Title: Developmental Variations in Corticostriatal Thalamocortical Circuits and their Relationship to Psychopathology.
Scope of Work: Use of advanced machine learning techniques to characterize the development of corticostriatal circuits using structural MRI and resting state functional fMRI, and to link variations in their trajectories to the emergence of psychopathology. This data reanalysis project involves the coordinated processing and analysis of MRI and behavioral data from many sites and investigative teams. Score: 24, 8th Percentile.
